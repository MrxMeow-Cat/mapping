Map Of Durian Production

Total Production Overview:

Johor consistently had the highest production of durians across the years, with the peak production in 2022 at 138,945.51 metric tons (mt). Pahang also showed high production numbers, following closely behind Johor, with its peak in 2022 at 122,290.97 mt. Kedah and Kelantan showed significant growth in production over the years, with notable increases from 2018 to 2022.

Highest Production by State and Year:

Johor had the highest production in every year from 2018 to 2022, consistently outproducing other states. The highest single year production for any state was in Johor in 2022 with 138,945.51 mt.

Trend Analysis:

Johor and Pahang show an upward trend in durian production, indicating potential for further growth. Kedah showed a remarkable increase in production from 2021 to 2022, nearly doubling its production. Selangor had an irregular pattern with a significant peak in 2021 but a sharp decline in 2022.

Comparison Across States:

States like Perlis and Labuan had the lowest production throughout the years. Sabah and Sarawak showed moderate production with some fluctuations but no significant increase or decrease over the years.
